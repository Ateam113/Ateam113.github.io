chocolate cake recipe chocolate chip brownies

  • Posted on
    September 18, 2018
  • Tags
    Delicious  |   Bakery
  • Comments
    12

This recipe is not for the fainthearted. It’s rich and buttery and oozes with two types of chocolate, making it rich and slightly gooey with a very chocolatey depth of flavour.

As with many of our chocolate cake recipes, these brownies contain more than 25 per cent chocolate, and the fact that we bake the equivalent of 3,000 chocolate bars every week should suffice as proof that this recipe is a hit.

Timing is crucial to the success of a brownie: a few minutes too long in the oven will make them rise and give them a spongy texture. It’s worth remembering that they continue to cook for a little while after being removed from the oven. Catch them earlier rather than late!

https://konditorandcook.com/blog/chocolate-cake-recipe-chocolate-chip-brownies/

With a bit of practice you will be able to create freshly baked brownies in less than 30 minutes. They are best eaten fresh, and delicious served with ice cream or a dollop of thick double cream.

For more easy cake recipes and advice on how to make cake, take a look at our Blog, including this Curly Whirly chocolate cake recipe.
